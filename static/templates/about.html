About ClubHub

ClubHub is a platform to streamline communication between Princeton clubs and interested students.

Students can search for clubs by keyword and by tags (such as A Cappella, Sports/Recreation, Political, and more!). Students can subscribe to a club to receive email notifications from officers. In addition, students can subscribe to entire tags of clubs to receive email notifications from all of the clubs under that tag.
